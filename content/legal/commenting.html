--- title: Commenting Policy & Terms date: 2021-09-18T21:12:44+02:00 draft: false heroimage: paragraph ---

Last updated: November 12, 2021

  • Comments are moderated and must be approved by the website owner before publication.
  • Controversial, critical and negative comments are welcome and will be approved to enable a diverse and lively exchange. Hence, the comments represent the expressions of the commentators and do not necessarily share the opinion of the website owner.
  • The website owner reserves the right not to publish or to delete comments which he considers inappropriate (in particular but not exclusively in the case of incitement to violence and hatred or racist content).
  • Providing an EMail address is optional. The address will not be revealed and only serves for identification purposes in case of a deletion request. It is not possible ask to for deletion of a comment if you cannot be properly identified as the originator of a comment.
