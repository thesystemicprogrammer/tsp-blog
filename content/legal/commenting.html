--- title: Commenting Policy & Terms date: 2021-09-18T21:12:44+02:00 draft: false heroimage: paragraph ---

Last updated: November 12, 2021

  • Comments are moderated and must be approved by the website owner before publication.
  • Controversial, critical and negative comments are welcome and will be approved to enable a diverse and lively exchange. Hence, the comments represent the expressions of the commentators and do not necessarily share the opinion of the website owner.
  • The website owner will not publish and delete illegal content and additionally reserves the right not to publish comments which he considers inappropriate.
  • Providing an EMail address is optional. The address will be stored as a hash value in the database and only serves to clearly identify the author of a comment in case of a deletion request. If you do not provide your EMail address, you waive your right to request deletion as it is not possible to clearly identify the originator.
