About me

Hi, I am Thomas Berchtold.

I have been working in the software industry for over 20 years in various roles such as programmer, designer, architect or requirements engineer. Currently I am working as a software engineer/coach/architect in transformation projects with a focus on the definition and implementation of cloud native architectures as well as the enabling of teams on new methods and technologies.

While I still love to code, I am particularly passionate about the social aspects of software engineering. How does the digital transformation change our job profiles, how can we quickly acquire necessary new skills or which forms of collaboration allow us to write better software? I consider the exploration of such types of questions to be paramount if we are to evolve and improve as software engineers.
